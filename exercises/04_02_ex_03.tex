{Water flows onto a flat surface at a rate of 5cm$^3$/s forming a circular puddle 10mm deep. How fast is the radius growing when the radius is:
	\begin{enumerate}
	\item		1 cm?
	\item		10 cm?
	\item		100 cm?
	\end{enumerate}
}
{\begin{enumerate}
\item	$5/(2\pi) \approx 0.796$cm/s
\item $1/(4\pi)\approx 0.0796$ cm/s
\item $1/(40\pi)\approx 0.00796$ cm/s
\end{enumerate}
}
