{$\ds \int \tanh x\ dx  = \ln (\cosh x) + C$}
{\hfill$\begin{aligned}[t]
		\int \tanh x\ dx &= \int \frac{\sinh x}{\cosh x}\ dx\\
											\text{Let $u = \cosh x$; $du = (\sinh x) dx$} \\	
											&= \int \frac{1}{u}\ du \\
											&= \ln |u| + C \\
											&= \ln (\cosh x) + C.
\end{aligned}$\hfill\null}
