{$\cosh 2x = \cosh^2x+\sinh^2x$}
{\hfill$\begin{aligned}[t]
		\cosh^2x+\sinh^2x &= \left(\frac{e^x+e^{-x}}{2}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{e^x-e^{-x}}{2}\right)^2 \\
											&= \frac{e^{2x} + 2 + e^{-2x}}{4} + \frac{e^{2x} - 2 + e^{-2x}}{4}\\
											&= \frac{2e^{2x}  + 2e^{-2x}}{4}\\
											&= \frac{e^{2x} + e^{-2x}}{2} \\
											&= \cosh 2x.			
\end{aligned}$\hfill\null}
