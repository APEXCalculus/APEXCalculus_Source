{$\ds \int \coth x\ dx  = \ln |\sinh x| + C$}
{\hfill$\begin{aligned}[t]
		\int \coth x\ dx &= \int \frac{\cosh x}{\sinh x}\ dx\\
											\text{Let $u = \sinh x$; $du = (\cosh x) dx$} 	\\
											&= \int \frac{1}{u}\ du \\
											&= \ln |u| + C \\
											&= \ln |\sinh x| + C.
\end{aligned}$\hfill\null}
